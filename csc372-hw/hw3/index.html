Finding The Freshest Local Eats Around Campus!

Attention Hungry Spartans!

Are you growing tired of visiting the cafe everyday? Looking for the perfect lunch or dinner spot within walking distance of campus? Well look no further! Here at Spartan Food Finder, we bring you a weekly catalog of various food & drink recommendations. Visit our meal plan builder to help you plan out some meals for the week!

Don Japanese Restaurant is a great choice for lunch or dinner as they offer a variety of authentic dishes. Not only is the food superb, but the staff are very friendly and have done a good job making a cozy and Japanese inspired setting within the restaurant. With good food as well as a friendly and cozy atmosphere, Don is sure to be your go-to Japanese restaurant when you're craving some warm ramen.
Don offers a delicious 6-piece serving of gyozas that can come either fried or steamed. The dish comes with a ginger sauce(which pairs very well with the fried option), though white sauce tends to be the sauce of choice when considering these dumplings. These are by far the best gyozas obtainable around campus!
There is quite the variety at Don when it comes to options of traditional Japanese soup. Whether your a fan of spicy ramen or beef udon, you're sure to find a bowl that will satisfy your cravings. Featured this week is a personal favorite- a bowl of shoyu ramen with chicken as it's protein.
Fajitas aren't the only meal that's served sizzling! Don has a selection of rice bowls to choose from. Bowls are served quite literally out of the pot, so you're guaranteed a hot, tasty meal!

Don Japanese Restaurant

Old Town Draught House holds a special place in the hearts of many. With daily drink specials, a friendly staff, and amazing pub food, it's no wonder why this is a staple for many students and locals alike! It's also worth notin that Old Town is practically a part of campus, what with it's semi-centralized location on campus right beside of Yum-Yum's and the Oakland parking deck.
Old Town's signature Smokehouse Burger isn't for the feint of heart. This large sandwich is packed with beef, bacon, and(you guessed it) barbecue sauce. As wrapped up as you'll be enjoying this burger, remember to try and not make a mess of your shirt!
Refreshing and light, Old Town's Caesar Salad is a great choice if you're looking for a lighter meal. If you aren't, Old Town offers a meal size option along with various protein to add(though chicken and caesar salad is a to-die-for combo).
A classic! Old Town provides a great take on the philly cheesesteak.
